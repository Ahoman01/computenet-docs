# ComputeNet Fair Launch Philosophy

ComputeNet follows a Bitcoin-style fair-launch path.

Principles:
- No premine
- No ICO
- No founder allocation
- No custody
- No investment offering
- Open source before genesis
- Public participation from genesis

The founder participates like any ordinary validator or miner.

Mainnet has not launched.

Current Status:
- Open Public Testnet v0.11
- Research preview only
